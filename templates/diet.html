Diabetes Diet Plan

It is recommended that a person with diabetes actively engage themselves in self-management, education, and treatment planning along with an individualized diet plan. With India being the diabetes capital of the world, it is high time that people with diabetes follow a diabetic diet chart since it significantly helps in maintaining blood glucose levels and alleviating symptoms of diabetes.The right food choices often help manage blood sugar levels, improve blood cholesterol, and keep you in a healthy weight range.

A person with diabetes can consume small and frequent meals to maintain their blood glucose level. Skipping meals may be disadvantageous since it may lead to hypoglycemia in a few people and for the same reason, a bedtime drink/snack is recommended since it prevents nocturnal hypoglycemia.
